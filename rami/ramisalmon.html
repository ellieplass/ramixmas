Special Salmon

This is best eaten on the floor, usually at girlfriend’s house because we both know she’s better at cooking salmon. Given the option between easy salmon and difficult salmon, she will choose difficult, and probably cry. But, it will be delicious.

Ingredients: Salmon, asparagus, potatoes, mushrooms. Champagne not optional. Grand Marnier optional.

Directions:

1. Cook salmon to perfect level.

2. Blend mashed potatoes. Roast asparagues. Sautee mushrooms.

3. Plate to a level worthy of a Michelin Star.

4. Eat on couch cushions with cardboard box for table. Comfort crying girlfriend (cooking is stressful). Cheers.
